## Introduction 

I remember when I started my GRE journey to build my profile for studying abroad, finding the right materials was a big challenge for me. Thankfully, a friend shared her amazing resources, and it was a great help. I believe the GRE is not super difficult. Because it's a standardized test, the question formats are always the same. As long as you understand the methods to solve them, you will do well.
